Here in this website you fill find the analysis that I conducted on the classic children’s book “Alice's Adventures in Wonderland” and on its first Italian translation!

Using AntiConc I analyzed the number of appearances and the relevant description of three secondary characters of this book: The “Hatter”, the “Rabbit” and the “March Hare”.

The goal of this study is to compare the original version with its Italian version in order to see if fidelity in the translation was maintained.

Check on the “Analysis” tab to know more!
